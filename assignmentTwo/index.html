About the candy

A little candy with a lot of personality

M&M's are small pieces of chocolate covered with a colourful candy shell. Their bright colours and familiar letter “m” make them easy to recognize.

They are enjoyed as a snack, shared at celebrations, added to desserts and used to make cookies, brownies and other sweet treats more colourful.
